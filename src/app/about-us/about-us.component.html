R30 philosophy

At La Dolce Vita, we're proud to embrace the 'R30 Philosophy' - a commitment to sourcing the freshest, most flavorful ingredients from local farmers within a 30-kilometer radius of our restaurant. We believe in supporting our community and reducing our carbon footprint by choosing to work with passionate local farmers and producers. Our dedication to the R30 Philosophy means that every mouthwatering dish on our menu is crafted with ingredients that are at the peak of their freshness. You can taste the difference in every bite, as our food bursts with flavor and a connection to the region we call home. Come and experience the essence of local, sustainable dining at its finest at La Dolce Vita.
